Dorian Janežič is a creative technologist interested in interactive experiences for cultural and educative applications. Originally trained as a digital communication specialist he has working experience in international companies, where he developed functional digital products.

He attended NYU Tisch School of the Arts IMA Low Res, a graduate program focusing on experimental and artistic uses of technology. Throughout the program he developed his own interdisciplinary practice which uses creative coding for generative design & 3D environments, spatial audio and interactive interfaces.
